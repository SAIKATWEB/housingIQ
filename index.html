About HousingIQ™

HousingIQ™ helps residential real estate buyers, sellers, lenders, and investors make more profitable decisions by providing timely, fact-based, and actionable insights into the US housing market. By blending econometric and machine learning techniques with data curated from disparate sources, HousingIQ’s proprietary platform gauges the impact of prevailing economic conditions on house prices. Currently, we publish 12-month house price appreciation forecasts for the national, state, and metro markets.
